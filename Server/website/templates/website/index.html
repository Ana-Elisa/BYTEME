About Us

We are a small development firm that focus heavily on the development and deployment of video games. Our aim is to create small project games and pass on the intellectual property of the game to a gaming company with a bigger name and reputation. Our current goal is to make the games we have enjoyable to our players and allow them to enjoy the game each in their own unique game. Not everyone is a hardore gamer, not everyone is competitve. But growth in a game is always visible and our company values that growth.

Duengeon Diver

BYTEME Studio will be producing a side-scroller video game. This single player combat game will have randomly generated levels that will generate new stages on every run through. This system will have players addicted to the ever-changing environment. The objective of the game is to successfully clear all levels without dying. There is a perma-death system meaning all items and current status of that playthrough will be deleted; however, progress such as achievements and unlocked items will be stored to that player's account. The inspiration of the game was to create a continuous game. The targeted platform will be for PC.
